InterMediate

educational institution that offers courses between secondary (high school) education and a full university degree. It is often known as a junior
college, community college, or intermediate college, depending on the country. These institutions usually offer 2-year programs or pre-university courses that prepare students for higher education. In many countries like Pakistan and India, "Intermediate" refers specifically to grades 11 and 12, which are also called first year and second year of college. .

Degree

.a higher education institution that offers academic programs leading to bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees. These universities provide in-depth education and training in various fields such as science, engineering, medicine, business, arts, and social sciences. Students typically enroll in a bachelor’s degree program, which usually lasts three to four years, and may later pursue postgraduate degrees like a master’s or Ph.D.

Post graduation

is an institution of higher education that offers advanced academic programs beyond the bachelor’s degree. These programs include master’s degrees (such as MA, MSc, MBA) and doctoral degrees (such as Ph.D.), which focus on specialized knowledge, research, and professional skills. Students usually join postgraduate studies after completing a bachelor's degree to deepen their understanding of a subject or to qualify for higher-level careers.
